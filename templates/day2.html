Svelte 2.0 - Day 2

1. Routing and Variable Rules:

Routes map URLs to specific functions in your application. Variable rules capture dynamic parts of the URL. Example: Route with a variable rule: @app.route('/user/<username>')

2. Understanding Templates:

Templates separate the presentation from the application logic. Use Jinja2 as the default templating engine in Flask. Store templates in a folder called "templates". Example: Render a template: render_template('index.html', title='Home')

3. Diving into Jinja2:

Jinja2 is a powerful templating engine integrated with Flask. It offers template inheritance, control structures, filters, and more. Example features: template inheritance, control structures, filters, variables, and expressions.
